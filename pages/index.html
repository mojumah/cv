  • Pharmaceuticals

    • It can be stressfull to see that your child is sick. The following medicines helped my child recover.

    1: Paracetamol: Calpol is available over the counter in the UK. It is a flavoured oral suspension and is used for the relife of:

      Fever
      Post-immunisation fever
      Cold & flu symptoms
      Teething
      Headache
      Sore throat
      Earache

    2: Coltrimazole & Hydrocortisone acetate: Canesten is an external use only cream and is used for the relife of:

      Nappy rash
      Athlete's foot
      Fungal infection of the breast fold (intertrigo)
